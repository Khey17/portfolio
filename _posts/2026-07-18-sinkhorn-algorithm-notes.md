I've been writing up notes from a Computational Optimal Transport reading seminar this summer, focused on entropic OT and the Sinkhorn algorithm.

The live notes live here: [khey17.github.io/sinkhorn-algorithm](https://khey17.github.io/sinkhorn-algorithm/).

There is a short recap of Monge and Kantorovich, a chapter on entropy and Sinkhorn updates, a runnable notebook, and an interactive ε slider so you can watch the plan go from blurry to sharp.

Code and sources are in [Khey17/sinkhorn-algorithm](https://github.com/Khey17/sinkhorn-algorithm). Main reference is Peyré and Cuturi, [arXiv:1803.00567](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.00567).
